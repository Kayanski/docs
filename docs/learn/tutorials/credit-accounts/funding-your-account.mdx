# Funding  your Account

In this guide, we will demonstrate how to successfully fund your credit account on the Mars dApp. Whether you are looking to increase your credit balance or deposit more assets, following these simple steps will ensure a smooth and secure transaction.

### Step 1: Accessing Credit Account Options

To get started with funding your account, click on the "Credit Account" tab located at the top of the webpage. In this section, find and select the "Fund" option to initiate the process of adding more assets to your account.

<img src={ Step1 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 2: Choosing an Asset

The next step involves selecting the type of asset you want to use for funding your account. Click on the "Select Asset" option to proceed and view the list of available assets.

<img src={ Step2 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

From the list of available assets, choose the specific assets you wish to deposit into your credit account. Be sure to review the assets' details before finalizing your selection.

<img src={ Step3 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 3: Setting the Amount

With the asset selected, the next phase involves determining the amount you want to deposit. Specify the quantity by entering the appropriate figure in the provided field.

<img src={ Step4 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 4: Transaction Confirmation

To finalize the process, you will need to sign and confirm the transaction. Follow the on-screen instructions to authenticate the transaction, ensuring the security and integrity of your account.

<img src={ Step5 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />
