# Withdrawing from your Account

In this guide, we will assist you in securely withdrawing funds from your account on the Mars dApp. By following these simple steps, you can easily manage and retrieve your assets whenever necessary. Let's get started:

### Step 1: Accessing the Withdrawal Option

Begin by navigating to the "Credit Account" tab, situated at the top of the webpage. Here, select the "Withdraw" option to start the process of withdrawing funds from your account.

<img src={ Step1 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 2: Asset Selection

Once in the withdrawal section, you will notice a dropdown list containing various assets. Select the specific asset you wish to withdraw by clicking on it from the dropdown list.

<img src={ Step2 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 3: Defining the Withdrawal Amount

After choosing the asset, determine the amount you want to withdraw. Input the desired quantity in the designated field to specify the exact amount you wish to retrieve.

<img src={ Step3 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 4: Transaction Authorization

To authorize the withdrawal, you will need to sign and confirm the transaction. Follow the prompts that appear on your screen to securely authenticate and approve the transaction.

<img src={ Step4 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 5: Withdrawal Confirmation

Once the transaction is completed, head over to the "Recent Transactions" tab to confirm the status of your withdrawal. This tab provides detailed information on all recent activities, including your latest withdrawal with links to a block exlorer. 

<img src={ Step5 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />
