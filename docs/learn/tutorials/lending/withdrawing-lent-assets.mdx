# Withdrawing Lent Assets

In this tutorial, we will guide you through the streamlined process of withdrawing lent assets on the Mars dApp. Withdrawing your lent assets allows you to reclaim them when necessary, with ease and efficiency. Letâ€™s dive into the steps:

### Step 1: Visit the Lend Page

Begin by navigating to the "Lend" page located under the "Earn" section on the Mars dApp to initiate the withdrawal process.

<img src={ Step1 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 2: Choosing an Asset to Withdraw 

In this step, choose an asset that you previously lent and are now looking to withdraw. Click on the "Withdraw" option adjacent to the asset to proceed.

<img src={ Step2 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 3: Defining the Withdrawal Amount

Now, specify the amount of the asset that you wish to withdraw. Ensure to double-check the amount before confirming the transaction.

<img src={ Step3 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 4: Transaction Authorization

Move ahead by authorizing the withdrawal transaction. Sign and confirm the transaction to initiate the withdrawal process securely.

<img src={ Step4 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 5: Completion and Notification

Congratulations! Upon successful transaction confirmation, you should receive a notification informing you that you've successfully reclaimed your assets from the lending pool.
