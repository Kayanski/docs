# Spot Trading

Welcome to this step-by-step guide where we will walk you through the process of executing spot trades on the Mars dApp. This tutorial is designed to help you navigate the trading features seamlessly, allowing for a smooth and secure trading experience. Let's get started:

### Step 1: Navigating to the Trade Tab

To commence your spot trading journey, head to the "Trade" tab available in the navigation menu. Once there, select the "Buy" tab to start setting up your trade.

<img src={ Step1 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 2: Selecting an Asset to Buy

Browse through the list of assets available on the platform. Once you have made your choice, select the asset you wish to buy to proceed.

<img src={ Step2 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 3: Selecting an Asset to Sell

Similarly, you need to select the asset you intend to sell in the trade. Scroll through the list and make your selection before moving on to the next step.

<img src={ Step3 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 4: Determining the Purchase Amount

Next, specify the amount of the asset you want to buy. You can input this value in the designated field, taking care to ensure accuracy to facilitate a smooth transaction.

<img src={ Step4 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 5: Transaction Confirmation

Before the trade is executed, you need to authorize the transaction. Do this by signing and confirming the transaction as per the instructions displayed on the screen.

<img src={ Step5 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 6: Monitoring Your New Balances

After the trade is completed, navigate to the bottom of the trade page. Here you will find real-time updates and tracking of your new asset balances.

<img src={ Step6 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 7: Utilizing the Account Health Feature

For a more detailed view of your account status, click on the "Account Health" button located on the right side of the page. This feature expands to provide an in-depth overview of your account, including current balances and other vital details.

<img src={ Step7 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 8: Checking Recent Transactions

Finally, to review your recent transactions, click on the "Wallet" tab. This section houses a detailed log of all your recent transactions, allowing for efficient tracking and management of your trades.

<img src={ Step8 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />
