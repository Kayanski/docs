# Connecting your Wallet

In this tutorial, we will walk you through the process of connecting your wallet to the Mars dApp seamlessly. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you navigate through the initial setup:

### Step 1: Visit the Mars dApp Website

Launch your preferred web browser and navigate to the official Mars dApp website using the following URL: https://marsprotocol.io/.

<img src={ Step1 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

Locate and click the "Connect Wallet" button, situated at the top right corner or by the trade button on the Trade page. This will lead you to the next phase of setting up your connection.

### Step 2: Review the Terms of Service

Before you proceed, take a moment to carefully read through the Terms of Service. This will give you a clear understanding of the protocol's policies and guidelines. Once you are comfortable with the terms, you can move forward to the next step.

<img src={ Step2 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

### Step 3: Choose a Supported Wallet

You will now be presented with a list of supported wallets. Choose a wallet that you already have set up and want to connect with the Mars dApp. 

<img src={ Step3 } style={{ marginBottom: 15 } } />

After selecting your wallet, follow the on-screen instructions to complete the connection. This process might slightly differ depending on the wallet you choose, but generally involves confirming the action in your wallet app or extension.
