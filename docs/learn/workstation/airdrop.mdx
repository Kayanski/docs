# Airdrop

### Step 1: Check if you qualify for the airdrop 

Before you claim the Mars airdrop, see if you qualify. You can find if you're address is whitelisted and the amount of your airdrop here: https://tinyurl.com/28zkzu2z/

### Step 2: Go to the Mars Airdrop website: https://airdrop.marsprotocol.io/  

<img src={AirdropStep2Url} />

Click on the "Connect a Terra Wallet" button. 

### Step 3: Connect a Terra wallet

<img src={AirdropStep3Url} />

For this tutorial, we will be using Terra Station. 

<Admonition type="tip" title="NOTE">
Some Mars wallets, like Falcon and Bitkeep, work with Mars but not Terra Classic. These wallets will not be able to be used to claim the airdrop. Supported wallets for the airdrop include: 
- Keplr (web)
- XDEFI (web)
- Leap (web)
</Admonition>

### Step 4: Confirm your airdrop amount

<img src={AirdropStep4Url} />

If your airdrop amount does not match your expected amount from the spreadsheet in Step 1, open a ticket with a Mars moderator through Discord. 

### Step 5: Connect a Keplr wallet

<img src={AirdropStep5Url} />

Keplr is the only supported wallet to claim the Mars airdrop. 

### Step 6: Sign message 

<img src={AirdropStep6Url} />

You will be prompted to sign the following message: 

`I am the owner of Terra account "terra..."; I wish to receive my aidrop of [ ] MARS at MARS account "mars..."
`

Select "Sign Message" to continue

### Step 7: Finalize claim

<img src={AirdropStep7Url} />

Select "Claim your Mars tokens now" to finalize your airdrop.

### Step 8: Congrats! You've claimed you're airdrop!

<img src={AirdropStep8Url} />

You can confirm your transaction using the Mars Block Explorer: http://explorer.marsprotocol.io/
